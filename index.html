Golf Tee Time Tracker

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Golf Tee Time Tracker helps users monitor golf-course availability and receive alerts when a tee time matches their saved search. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information, including email address and mobile phone number.
  • Saved tee-time search preferences, such as course, date, time range, player count, and hole preference.
  • Notification delivery records and basic operational logs used to maintain and troubleshoot the service.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to operate Golf Tee Time Tracker, monitor requested tee-time availability, send requested transactional alerts, prevent duplicate notifications, troubleshoot problems, and improve reliability.

SMS Messaging

Users receive SMS alerts only after expressly opting in. Messages are transactional tee-time availability alerts related to searches created by the user. Message frequency varies based on matching availability. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase.

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text-message opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties except service providers that support delivery of the requested messages.

Service Providers

We may use service providers to operate the service, including hosting, database, email, and SMS delivery providers. These providers receive information only as needed to perform services on our behalf. SMS delivery is currently supported by Twilio.

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for third-party advertising. We may disclose information when required by law, to protect the service or its users, or to service providers acting on our behalf.

Data Retention And Security

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, and resolve operational issues. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

Users may stop SMS messages by replying STOP. Users may also request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Golf Tee Time Tracker is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy as the service changes. The effective date above will be updated when material revisions are made.
